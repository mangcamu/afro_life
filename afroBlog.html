WHY ART IN THE WORK PLACE?

• COMPANY CULTURE- When done right art in the workplace can express the personal
values and culture of the organisation but also have the following benefits:

• Art is an oppotunity for companies to help clients, visitors and employees get to know
and get a feel for who the organisation is.

• Art in the workplace encourages and promotes social interactions. It stimulates
emotional responses and fosters personal relationships.

• Art in the workplace effects how the people within the environment feel about
themselves and the company for which they work.

• Art opens minds, broadens horizons, challenges fixed mindsets and facilitates learning.

•Art in the workplace enhances motivation and increases productivity.
